TOP ARCADE QUESTIONS

Having problems downloading your games?

Sometimes it takes a few seconds for your download to start. If your download does not start shortly, try one of the following options:

Option 1:
Go to the \\Program Files\Real\RealArcade\ folder where you have RealArcade installed and look for a file called tmp.rgp. If that file exists, delete it, restart RealArcade, and try your download again.

OR

Option 2:
Clear your Temporary Internet files inside of Internet Explorer:

  1. Start Internet Explorer
  2. Click the "Tools" menu.
  3. Click "Internet Options"
  4. Click "Delete Files"*
  5. Try your download again

*Note: This operation may take a few minutes to complete.

If you need more help, please contact Technical Support.
