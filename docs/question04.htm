TOP ARCADE QUESTIONS

How do I get my free monthly GamePass game?

If you are a GamePass Member, you are given a "credit" for a free game each month. To use this credit, click "Buy Full Version Now" on the Game Page of the game you would like to purchase. You will then have the option of using your GamePass credit for that month, or taking a $5 discount off the listed price instead.
