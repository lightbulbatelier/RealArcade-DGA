How does GamePass work?

Every month, GamePass members get a GamePass FREE GAME credit, which works like a gift certificate. After clicking the button to buy a game, GamePass members are offered the option to use their GamePass Credit to get the game for FREE, or to get £3 off the current price. How do I purchase games using my monthly GamePass credit and the 5 € discount?

Just buy any game as you normally would through RealArcade:

  1. Decide which game you want to purchase.
  2. Click the "Buy Full Version Online" button on the game page in RealArcade, and you will be asked to sign in to your account.
  3. Once signed in, you'll be recognized as a GamePass member and offered the option to use your GamePass Credit to get the game for FREE, or to apply your £3 GamePass discount on the retail price of the game (this option is offered to you during Step 2 of the order process in the specially-highlighted "GamePass Benefits" section).
  4. After you have chosen to use your GamePass Credit or £3 Discount, the new purchase price will be reflected in your order total.
  5. Click the "Next Step" button to confirm your order.
  6. Download the game, install it, and have fun!

After you have used up your monthly GamePass free game credit, any additional games purchased that month will receive a £3.00 discount automatically (the discount will be shown in Step 2 of the order process).

If I cancel my GamePass subscription, am I able to keep the games I bought with my credits?

Yes. The games you buy with your credits are yours to keep forever, even if you cancel your GamePass subscription.

Does my monthly GamePass credit expire?

Your monthly GamePass credit will expire two months after the date granted.
