GamePass: More Fun, Less Money!
  What is GamePass, and how does it work?
RealArcade GamePass is a monthly subscription service. Members of GamePass pay a low monthly fee of only £5.95 and are entitled to choose and own any one of our great full version games (Up to a £12.99 value) each month, in addition members also receive £3.00 off all other game purchases. Join GamePass Now!

How much does GamePass cost?
GamePass is only £5.95 a month. Members of GamePass get our RealArcade player, a free game credit every month and £3 off every other game you buy.

Which games can I buy using my GamePass credit?
You can choose from any game in our catalog up to £12.99. We have over 300 amazing game titles to choose from and growing. As a GamePass member you get a game credit every month, and can get £3 off any other game purchase. Download the games immediately and own them forever!

If I cancel my GamePass subscription am I able to keep the games I bought with my credits?
Yes. The games you buy with your credits are yours to keep forever - even if you cancel your GamePass subscription.

How do I purchase games using my monthly GamePass credit and the £3 discount?
Just buy any game as you normally would through RealArcade, the real.com games or the RealNetworks website. After you login, you will be presented with the option to use your game credit or the £3.00 discount. After you use your monthly credit any additional games purchased in that month will receive a £3.00 discount automatically.

What happens if I do not use my GamePass credit by the end of the billing month?
All credits given during the current billing month must be used within that period; unused credits expire at the end of the current billing month.

Is there a minimum commitment required when I sign up for GamePass?
There is no minimum commitment. You may cancel anytime by contacting RealNetworks Customer Service.

Can I cancel my GamePass subscription at any time?
Yes. Members can cancel their GamePass subscription at any time. GamePass benefits will continue through the last paid period.
